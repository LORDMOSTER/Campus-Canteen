# 🎓 Campus Canteen — Student App

Welcome to the **Campus Canteen Student Portal**! This app is designed to make your college dining experience faster, smarter, and more convenient. 

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Simple Security (Auth)
- **Easy Sign In**: Log in with your email and password.
- **New Account**: Create a new profile in seconds.
- **Forgot Password**: If you forget your password, you can reset it safely using a 6-digit OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your email.

### 2. Smart Menu Browsing
- **Categories**: Browse food by categories like "Rice & Noodles," "Snacks," "Beverages," and more.
- **Real-time Stock**: You can see exactly how many items are left in stock before you order.
- **Live Search**: Find your favorite dish instantly by typing its name.

### 3. AI-Powered Budget Mode 💡
Not sure what to eat? Enter your budget (for example, ₹100), and our **AI Advisor** will suggest the best meal combinations for you!
- It looks at what's popular, what's in stock, and what you've ordered before.
- It gives you multiple "Combos" (like a main dish + a drink) that fit perfectly within your budget.

### 4. Seamless QR Payments 💳
We use **Razorpay** for safe and secure digital payments.
- **How it works**:
  1. Add items to your cart.
  2. Click "Pay" and complete the transaction.
  3. Once successful, the app instantly generates a **unique QR Code**.
  4. Show this QR code at the canteen counter to collect your food. No more waiting for paper receipts!

### 5. Order Tracking & History
- **Active Orders**: See your current orders and their status in real-time.
- **Order History**: Keep track of everything you've ordered in the past, including dates and prices.

### 6. Personalized Profile
- **Profile Picture**: Upload your photo to personalize your account.
- **Quick Stats**: See your total orders and spending habits at a glance.

---

## 🛠️ How it Works (Concepts)

### **The "Budget Thing" (AI Recommendations)**
Instead of just showing random items, we use a "Generative AI" model. It understands that someone who wants a "Fried Rice" might also want a "Juice." It calculates the total price and ensures you get the best "Value for Money" without going over your limit.

### **The QR Code System**
Your QR code is your digital token. It contains your order details encrypted inside. When the vendor scans it, the system automatically marks your order as "Completed" and updates the canteen's stock levels. It's the ultimate paperless system!

### **Live Updates**
The app is "Live." This means if someone else buys the last plate of Biryani, your screen will update instantly to show it's "Out of Stock," so you never place an order for something that isn't there.

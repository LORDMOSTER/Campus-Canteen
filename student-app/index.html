📋 Compliance & Privacy

Terms of Service

By using the Campus Canteen app, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate account information
  • Use the service for legitimate food orders only
  • Respect the canteen staff and fellow students
  • Pay for all orders placed through your account
  • Not share your account credentials with others

🔒 Privacy Policy

We collect and use your information to:

  • Process your food orders and payments
  • Communicate order status updates
  • Improve our service based on feedback
  • Maintain account security

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Payment processing is handled securely by Razorpay.

💰 Refund Policy

Refunds are provided in the following cases:

  • Order cancelled before preparation begins
  • Food quality issues (case-by-case basis)
  • System errors resulting in duplicate charges

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
